Welcome to SHANTI NIKETAN GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS

Shanti Niketan Group of Institutions was established in the year 2009 under the aegis of Shanti Niketan Trust comprising of renowned academicians, business professionals and social activists. The institute is located at Goon-Gejha Road, Ikla, Mohiuddinpur, just 1 km. away from NH-58, (just after Modinagar, Ghaziabad) and 45 minutes, drive from Delhi. The main attraction of SNGI is its huge workshop and labs with all the modern equipments. The institute boosts its strength by practical delivery of the subjects for better learning and understanding of concepts.
